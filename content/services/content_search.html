Global Relay Search gives your organization the ability to harness and profit from your communications data. It uses flexible role-based access controls to provide every employee with appropriate access to the data stored in Global Relay Archive, as well as the workflow tools they need to do their jobs.

From a compliance officer conducting daily communications review, to in-house counsel conducting eDiscovery, to a business user who needs to locate a critical email to make a decision, Global Relay Search offers every employee a tailored, intuitive UI to help them work more efficiently.
