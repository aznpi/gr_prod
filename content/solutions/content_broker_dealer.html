Overview

SEC Rules 17a-3 and 17a-4, combined with FINRA Rules 3110 and 4511, require Broker-Dealers to implement a compliance archiving and monitoring solution to retain and supervise electronic communications. These requirements extend to all written business-related communications, including email, instant messaging, Bloomberg®, Thomson Reuters, mobile messaging, and social media.
