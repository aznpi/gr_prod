Meet regulatory requirements with an integrated compliance archiving and messaging platform.

Overview

As email, IMs, and other electronic message types continue to grow as the principal tools of business communication, compliance has never been a more complicated process to manage. In the highly regulated financial industry, the requirements for compliant messaging and recordkeeping are especially stringent.

Global Relay solutions are specifically designed to meet the compliance archiving, monitoring, audit, privacy, and business continuity requirements of financial firms and public companies. Our archive, search, and message systems have integrated compliance features as well as unparalleled supervisory capabilities. Bringing all your organization’s electronic messages under our unified platform gives you a streamlined system for fast, simple, and airtight compliance.

Benefits

Proactive Preparation

  • Avoid fines – Our integrated compliance system is designed to meet the requirements of all major regulatory bodies
  • Always be ready for potential audits – Our eDiscovery tools allow for extensive search and retrieval in minutes (if not seconds), and legal holds ensure the retention of all relevant materials

Precise Regulatory Compliance

  • Receive automatic notification of infractions – Customizable lexicons and exclusions create flagging rules that are perfectly tailored to your company
  • Don’t miss any messages – Capture all channels that staff are using to communicate
  • Get professional support during audits from our experienced audit and eDiscovery teams

Efficient Workflow

  • Easily and dependably back up, retrieve, and export data
  • Grant limited temporary access to third-party examiners
  • Get a clear picture of reviewer and end-user activity with extensive reporting features
  • Efficiently review, flag, and escalate messages via a compliance dashboard
  • Manage cases using a workflow – organize data with folders, labels, and reviewer-defined priorities, then grant access to specific users and groups
  • Work independently with an intuitive interface – no need to rely on IT
