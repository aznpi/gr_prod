Proactively prepare for litigation and eDiscovery.

Overview

Ever since financial firms and public companies were mandated to preserve all their business communications – including electronic messages – as part of beefed-up compliance rules, organizations have been scrambling to manage a daily torrent of emails, IMs, text messages, and other electronic message types. To produce evidentiary quality records in the short timeframe of an eDiscovery request without incurring huge legal costs, organizations need a comprehensive, easily navigable archive.

Global Relay systems are designed for eDiscovery. We provide efficient online tools for collaboration, case management, and responses to legal data requests. With Global Relay, your firm's data is always on file – retrievable and litigation-ready within seconds.

Benefits

Have all the data you need, when you need it

  • Maintain evidentiary quality copies of every message
  • Easily export data for evidence submission
  • Find any message in seconds – unparalleled search performance and precision tools ensure you can find that important message when you need it
  • Keep your important messages in a data safe zone – both of our data centers are in Canada, under strong privacy laws, and free from Patriot Act information requests

Protect your company with strong legal processes

  • Mark messages as attorney-client privileged or retain them with Legal Holds
  • Establish an unbiased chain of custody maintained by an independent third party

Stay compliant with regulations

  • Get expert assistance during audits – our specialists help customers facing audits and litigation every day
  • Store data from multiple jurisdictions without issues – we have customers in 90 countries, and are well-versed in cross-border data transfer laws
