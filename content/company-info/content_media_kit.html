Global Relay Communications Inc. Global Relay is the leading provider of cloud-based electronic message archiving, supervision, and eDiscovery solutions for the global financial sector, delivering services to 18,000 customers in 90 countries. Global Relay Archive securely captures and preserves email, instant messaging, mobile messaging, Bloomberg, Thomson Reuters, Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook and more — with Blackberry, iPhone, iPad, Android, Outlook and Web access. Global Relay has developed partnerships with a select group, committed to aligning technology and expertise to jointly deliver integrated enterprise solutions and add value to our customers. Global Relay is the archive back end of Thomson Reuters Messaging Compliance (TRMC), delivering services for Thomson Reuters customers worldwide. Over 60 of the world's best Hosted Exchange providers enable message archiving for their customers via technology partnerships with Global Relay. In addition, Global Relay is the exclusive Message Archiving Vendor in FINRA's Compliance Resource Provider Program.

Experience. Global Relay has provided hosted message archiving services to the financial sector for over 15 years. Today, we are the leading provider of compliance message archiving services to the financial world. Global Relay’s 20,000 customers in 90 countries include investment advisors, private equity funds, 40% of FINRA broker-dealers, 70% of SEC hedge funds, and 22 of the world’s top 25 banks. We serve financial service firms worldwide that are regulated by the SEC/FINRA, IIROC, FCA, AMF and many others. We also work with the hedge funds of many prime brokerages, such as Goldman Sachs, UBS, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley and Credit Suisse.

Rock-solid Reliability. Global Relay develops, owns and operates its services on a fully-managed, outsourced model with East coast/West coast SOC audited data centers. Our systems are extremely reliable and cost-effective. All data archived with Global Relay has end-to-end security, encrypted with a dual encryption algorithm system using National Security Agency level AES and RSA encryption keys ensuring the highest level of data security available today.

High Performance Systems. The technology that powers Global Relay Archive and the monitoring systems is second to none. Our systems have unmatched scalability and are able to index terabytes of data with no performance issues. Where most archiving systems are based solely on database technology, Global Relay's system is based on a hybrid database/search engine core. A typical online search is less than three seconds. This translates into unsurpassed productivity while conducting search, retrieval and monitoring activities regardless of company size.

Archive Unification. Global Relay Archive enables firms to unify all of their electronic messages in the cloud in a single repository with federated search capabilities. Global Relay Archive supports all major message types used in finance, including email (from virtually any platform), public instant messaging (AIM, MSN, Yahoo!, GoogleTalk), public social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook), enterprise social media (Chatter, Yammer), mobile messaging (Blackberry and Android), Bloomberg, Thomson Reuters, CME/Pivot, ICE/YellowJacket, Jabber/XMPP, WebEx, OCS/Lync, LivePerson, OpenFire, FactSet, Web and Global Relay Message.

The Company. Global Relay is a privately-held, financially sound, federal corporation with 100% growth year after year. All of Global Relay's messaging systems are proprietary and owned and operated by Global Relay. KPMG annually provides unique and extensive validation of Global Relay's internal business, operational and security controls.

Superior Service Team. Global Relay's team of dedicated professionals has the highest synergy of business, technical and legal expertise. Our fully-managed systems alleviate your company's burden of electronic record preservation, management, backup and storage. We have a full time in-house legal compliance department that will ensure all regulatory compliance, electronic discovery and audit requirements are met. Our technology support technicians are experienced and accessible. This is all part of our commitment to provide the best balance of service, support, quality and affordability.
