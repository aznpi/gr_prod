Working in the Office

Working in the Office

We know how important a good office environment is to having happy and productive employees. On top of all the great amenities that make our friends jealous, we have a spacious open-plan office, with lots of breakout rooms for collaboration or independent work. Our headquarters is in beautiful Gastown, known for its historic cobblestone streets, delicious restaurants, and hip boutiques. We’re easy to get to no matter how you commute – we even have a bike cage and showers to accommodate our many cycling enthusiasts.

GR Bistro

GR Bistro

One of the things that get us excited to come to work in the morning is the prospect of a delicious lunch prepared by our in-house chef. His specialties include pulled pork sandwiches, red curry with prawns, and chicken souvlaki. Eat in the lounge, or take your plate up to our rooftop patio, which overlooks downtown Vancouver and the Burrard Inlet. In the mornings, the lounge is fully stocked with complimentary bagels and toast, with snacks and drinks available throughout the day. At the end of a workday, you might find some staff enjoying a glass of one of the local craft beers that we keep on tap.

The GR Gastown Grand Prix

The Global Relay Gastown Grand Prix

The Global Relay Gastown Grand Prix is North America’s favorite criterium (circuit-style race). The 1.2km cobblestone route has a long and rich history in Gastown — Vancouver’s oldest and most characteristic neighbourhood. With an annual turnout of thousands of cheering spectators, riders and fans love the Gastown race for its challenging course, spectacular finishes and the sheer energy of the huge crowds.

In 2012, after the race had been on hiatus for several years due to lack of funding, Global Relay stepped in to bring back the race. Global Relay’s involvement with the race is unique. Far beyond just writing a cheque, we made the strategic decision to not only fund, but also manage the event. more info

WHAT YOU GET

Career

"Landing here was one of the best things to happen to me in my professional career and helped realize a dream I had let go of long ago," says Media Production Specialist David Ermacora. Global Relay works with 20,000 customers in 90 countries – and we’re just getting started. Working for Global Relay gives you the opportunity to develop cutting-edge technology, and interact with some of the world’s most powerful organizations.

Voice

We believe in harnessing the knowledge and expertise of every single employee to help us be the best in our industry. Global Relay has a flat organizational structure, giving all employees the opportunity to share ideas and initiatives with the senior leadership team.

Collaboration

We may have grown to a workforce of hundreds of employees, but our entrepreneurial spirit is alive and well. Global Relay’s offices are designed for collaboration, with an open floor plan, breakout rooms, and lots of common spaces. On both the technology side and the business side of the company, teams regularly work together to solve complex problems.

Competitive Compensation

"Global Relay understands that happy employees are productive employees. That's a very rare thing in the IT community. Global Relay provides the support to let us focus on what we do best," says Java Developer Kate Bateman. In addition to competitive salaries, Global Relay employees receive a top-of-the-line benefits package with extended medical. Regular bonuses are awarded based on company revenue, ensuring that the growing success of the company is directly shared with the people that help make it happen.

Learning & Development

Global Relay is a rapidly growing company, which offers employees the opportunity to evolve their roles within the organization. Many of our employees have taken on new challenges by moving to a different team within the company, assuming a leadership position, or joining one of our international offices. All new employees go through a comprehensive onboarding and training program, giving them the necessary knowledge to start making contributions right away.

Growth

Joining Global Relay offers ambitious and talented professionals the opportunity to grow with the company as the results of their hard work are reflected in the success of the business. We have grown every year since we were founded in 1999, to the point where we now compete against tech giants like IBM, Symantec and HP. We have the ambitious goal of becoming a billion-dollar company, and we need the absolute best people to help us reach it.
