Security & Encryption

Echoworx and Global Relay have joined forces to provide a complete end-to-end encrypted email and data loss prevention (DLP) solution in a Software-as-a-Service delivery model. Encrypted Mail Gateway protects emails in transit being shared over the Internet, while fully integrating with the Global Relay Archive's dual AES/RSA encryption for archived data at rest. As a policy-based encryption solution, emails containing sensitive information are automatically encrypted to be in compliance with privacy and security regulations (SOX, HIPAA, GLBA, PCI DSS, Massachusetts Privacy Law), without interrupting employees' day to day workflow.

Cloudmark's industry leading spam and virus detection software is a critical component of Global Relay's multi-layered, continuously updated, email spam and virus filtering system. All email is scanned by Cloudmark's Advanced Message Fingerprinting to detect spam, phishing, and viruses in real time using algorithm patterns. To protect and adapt to new categories of threats, a cache of threat fingerprint patterns is updated every 30-60 seconds based on worldwide feedback loops and the latest spammer tactics.
